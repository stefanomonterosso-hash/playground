WorkRamp is Omio’s learning platform. You’ll use it from your first days in training and throughout your time here — not only for onboarding, but for ongoing learning whenever we ship new guides or refreshers.

Open WorkRamp Browse libraries

During training

Training is organised in modules. Each module has related guides — with different kinds of media (for example video, articles, walkthroughs) and knowledge checks so you can confirm you’ve understood the material.

  • New joiners are assigned guides as they move through the programme.
  • That path continues through to the final quiz — still inside WorkRamp.

After training — learning doesn’t stop

WorkRamp stays part of life at Omio. You’ll see new assignments over time: refreshers, or brand-new guides when we add providers, change processes, or roll out something the whole team needs to know.

Libraries — come back anytime

Even after a guide is marked complete, you can always open Libraries: that’s where guides live in one place so you can rewatch a video, re-read a process, or double-check how something works — whenever you need it on the job.

Go to Libraries

WorkRamp opens in a new tab and uses your Omio access. If you can’t sign in, use the same support channel your team uses for workplace tools (e.g. IT or your lead).
