LMS & in-house training

The majority of the content in the LMS is built and maintained by us. We have a long history of in-house training: we’ve trained a strong Tier 2 team of experts in Berlin, and we keep shaping how Omio learns and upskills.

2025 and our BPO partner journey

In 2025 we doubled down on our BPO partner work — making sure training everywhere mirrors Omio standards.

  • El Salvador — we worked with Telus so their training reflected our bar.
  • South Africa — we actually went to South Africa to train the first agents from Nutun and accredited their trainers.
  • Yoummday — a fully remote BPO; we trained the team and accredited their trainer.
  • Japan — we recently onboarded our new BPO partner, Receipt Roller.
